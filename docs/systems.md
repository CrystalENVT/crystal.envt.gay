## Naming Schemes

I've taken to naming my devices in 1 of 2 manners.

1) Thaumcraft Aspects (Resources included below) - For most devices
    1) [Thaumcraft 4](https://ftbwiki.org/List_of_Aspects_(Thaumcraft_4))
    2) [Thaumcraft 5](https://ftbwiki.org/List_of_Aspects_(Thaumcraft_5))
2) Types of Pies - For specifically my Raspberry Pis

3) [Cognito](https://ftbwiki.org/List_of_Aspects_(Thaumcraft_5)#Tier_3) - *Mind, Memory, Cognition* - Home Assistant
4) [Potentia](https://ftbwiki.org/List_of_Aspects_(Thaumcraft_5)#Tier_3) - *Energy* - Stream system
5) [Telum](https://ftbwiki.org/List_of_Aspects_(Thaumcraft_4)#Tier_7) - *Weapon, Attack, Harm* - Gaming system
6) [Motus](https://ftbwiki.org/List_of_Aspects_(Thaumcraft_5)#Tier_1) - *Motion, Animation* - Laptop
7) [Iter](https://ftbwiki.org/List_of_Aspects_(Thaumcraft_4)#Tier_2) - *Travel, Journey* - Phone
